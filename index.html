Frequently Asked Questions?

Find answers to common questions that come in your mind related to IPO.

How to Subscribe to an IPO?
  • Step 1: Login to your respective service provider.
  • Step 2: Click on the IPO button.
  • Step 3: Select the IPO you want to bid and enter the relevant details.
  • Step 4: Your subscription will be completed once you make the payment or give permission.
Should I buy an IPO first day?
  • The decision to buy an IPO on the first day depends on your investment strategy and research.
How do you know if an IPO is good?
  • Research the company's financials, management team, and market potential.
  • Check the demand for the IPO and expert opinions.
How to check IPO start date?
  • Check the stock exchange's official website or your broker's platform.
What is issue size?
  • Issue size refers to the total value of shares being offered in the IPO.
How many shares in a lot?
  • A lot is a fixed number of shares defined by the company, and it varies for each IPO.
How is the lot size calculated?
  • Lot size is calculated based on the share price and the minimum investment amount required.
Who decides the IPO price band?
  • The price band is decided by the company's management in consultation with the underwriters.
What is IPO GMP?
  • GMP stands for Grey Market Premium, which indicates the premium at which shares are trading in the grey market before listing.
How many lots should I apply for IPO?
  • You should apply for the number of lots based on your investment capacity and the IPO's potential.
